Mini-Game Collection

Click on the image and it will take you to the game.

The goal of this project is to provide an engaging experience for users through a collection of interactive mini-games. It was made with p5.js and its overarching theme is to challenge and entertain the user with diverse tasks, without any instructions and see how long it takes them to complete it. Within this project, users will encounter a variety of captivating challenges, from arranging facial features to solving mazes and dynamic puzzles. The theme revolves around fostering creativity and problem-solving skills, encouraging users to navigate through each mini-game intuitively. Without explicit instructions, the objective is to prompt spontaneous exploration, allowing users to discover the rules and mechanics organically. Engaging with this piece involves interacting with on-screen elements such as dragging and dropping, clicking, and utilizing keyboard controls, ensuring a dynamic and enjoyable experience for first-time users.
